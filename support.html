Sprout Fortress Support

Sprout Fortress is an offline iPhone tower defense game. For help, please contact the developer through the App Store product page after release.

Common Questions

  • Does the game need internet? No. Normal gameplay is offline.
  • Where is progress stored? Progress is stored locally on your device.
  • Does the game include purchases or ads? No. This build has no ads, analytics, login, or in-app purchases.
