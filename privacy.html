Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 14, 2026

Sprout Fortress is a pure offline single-player iOS game. The app does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

Information Collection

Sprout Fortress does not collect personal information, account information, contact information, location data, photos, advertising identifiers, or device analytics.

Local Game Progress

The app may store gameplay progress and settings locally on your device. This information remains on the device and is not transmitted to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app does not include advertising SDKs, analytics SDKs, tracking SDKs, social login, in-app purchases, or third-party purchase services.

Children's Privacy

The game is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Support

For support, please contact the developer through the App Store product page after release.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published on this page.
